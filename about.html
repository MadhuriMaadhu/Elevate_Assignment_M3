Frontend Technologies

Backend and front end technologies are the two main categories that make up a web application. A web application is a piece of software that runs on a server and provides functionality to users, such as sending email or creating online forms. While front end technologies deal with the appearance, a backend technology is what powers the user interface—how it’s built and maintained.

Factors to Consider the Right Front End Technology

Choosing the right front end technology is one of the most important decisions you'll make as a software developer. The technology stack you choose will affect everything from how your app looks to how it performs, so it's important that you pick a toolset that's going to be able to grow with your business. When choosing a front end technology, consider these factors:-

  1. Skill level of your team
  2. Cost of implementing each option
  3. Each option's ability to scale with your business needs
  4. Learning curve
  5. How well each option aligns with your company's mission statement
  6. Also, consider your long-term goals and the kind of product or service you want to provide
